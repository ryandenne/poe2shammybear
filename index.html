Ascendancy order (Shaman)

1st point

Druidic Champion

Required gateway node. No effect on its own — take it to unlock Furious Wellspring.

2nd point — most important

Furious Wellspring

Disables Rage degeneration entirely. Adds passive Rage regeneration scaled by Mana Regen on gear. You start every boss at full Rage and Glory. This is what makes the build work.

3rd point

Reactive Growth

Grants Adaptations — reduces elemental damage taken based on what's hitting you. Strong defensive layer, pairs well with face tanking.

4th point

Avatar of Evolution

Converts 5% of physical damage taken as Fire/Cold/Lightning (15% total). Makes getting hit build your elemental defences — rewards the aggressive playstyle.

Leveling phases

Campaign (lvl 1–51) — before Walking Calamity

Shift into Bear form immediately. Use Maul to generate Rage, Furious Slam for big packs, Rampage to move through zones. Cast Volcano in human form before shifting — it keeps erupting while you brawl. Rush Furious Wellspring at your first Ascendancy trial.

Post-lvl 52 — Walking Calamity unlocked

The build clicks into place. Maul to build Rage → overflow to Glory → activate Walking Calamity → meteors fall while you keep attacking. Never leave Bear form. Stop for every Ezomyte Remnant to stack Runic Ward on your gear.

Don't forget

Stop at every Ezomyte Remnant in each zone — loot Verisium and craft Runic Ward onto helmet, body armour, gloves, and boots. It's a free second health pool unique to 0.5 and perfectly fits the face-tank identity of this build.
