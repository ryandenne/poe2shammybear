Ascendancy — Shaman

Take these in order. The 2nd point (Furious Wellspring) is the most important node in the entire build — rush your first Ascendancy trial to get it.

1
Druidic Champion
Required gateway node. No direct benefit for this build — take it only to unlock Furious Wellspring.
2
Furious Wellspring ★
Disables Rage degeneration entirely and adds passive Rage regeneration scaled by your Mana Regeneration stat. This means you start every boss encounter at full Rage and Glory — Walking Calamity is ready before the fight even begins. This is what makes the build work. Prioritise Mana Regen on jewelry as a result.
3
Reactive Growth
Grants Adaptations that reduce elemental damage taken based on what element is hitting you. Strong defensive layer that rewards face-tanking — the more consistently you get hit by one element, the more durable you become against it.
4
Avatar of Evolution
Converts 5% of physical damage taken as each of Fire, Cold, and Lightning (15% total). Getting hit by physical attacks now builds your Adaptation stacks for all elements simultaneously. Perfectly rewards the aggressive, face-tank playstyle.

Leveling phases

Campaign · Levels 1–51
Bear brawler
Shift into Bear form immediately and stay there. Use Maul to generate Rage, Furious Slam for big packs, and Rampage to move through zones quickly. Cast Volcano in human form before every major fight — it keeps erupting while you brawl as a Bear. Rush your first Ascendancy trial and take Furious Wellspring immediately.
Post level 52
Walking Calamity unlocked — build clicks into place
The full identity arrives. Maul to build Rage → overflow to Glory → activate Walking Calamity → meteors fall while you keep attacking. Never leave Bear form. Make sure you're stopping at every Ezomyte Remnant to stack Runic Ward on your gear as you find Verisium.
Endgame
Optimisation — Talisman, gem levels, rare upgrades
Focus on gem levels after completing the campaign. Upgrade Talisman to best-in-slot physical DPS. Look for the "Gain extra damage as Physical" modifier — it's a massive multiplier. Target Defiance of Destiny amulet as your first premium upgrade for survivability.

Boss rotation

Simple and consistent. The build rewards aggression — staying in melee range is both the offensive and defensive strategy.

  1. Pre-cast Volcano in human form before entering the boss arena. It keeps erupting automatically while you're in Bear form.
  2. Shift to Bear and activate Walking Calamity immediately — thanks to Furious Wellspring, you start the fight at full Glory.
  3. Spam Maul at point-blank range. Stay on top of the boss. Multiple meteors will overlap on the hitbox for massive damage.
  4. Reactivate Walking Calamity as soon as it expires. Keep Rage and Glory building continuously — never back off.
  5. Use Furious Slam during any downtime windows between Calamity activations for additional burst.

Runic Ward (0.5 mechanic)

Don't skip this. It's free and it's one of the best defensive layers in 0.5 for a face-tank build.

Every zone in 0.5 contains Ezomyte Remnants. Interacting with them drops Verisium currency. Use Verisium to craft Runic Ward onto your helmet, body armour, gloves, and boots. Runic Ward acts as a second health pool that sits on top of your regular life pool — enemies have to chew through it before touching your actual health. Combined with high Armour and Reactive Growth Adaptations, this makes the build extremely durable in the campaign and early maps.

Tips and common mistakes

✓
Upgrade your Talisman constantly
The Talisman is your damage. An under-levelled Talisman is the #1 reason the build feels slow. Check vendors every act and replace it the moment you see better physical DPS.
✓
Mana Regen on jewellery is a damage stat
Furious Wellspring scales with Mana Regeneration — the more you have, the faster passive Rage Regen ticks. This directly affects how quickly you reach max Glory and start dropping meteors.
✗
Don't stack Energy Shield
ES recharge delay was nerfed hard in 0.5. Bear Shaman has no ES scaling. Any ES on gear is wasted item budget — prioritise Life and Armour instead.
✓
Walking Calamity hits harder on large bosses
The meteors land somewhat randomly within a large area. On big boss hitboxes multiple meteors overlap simultaneously — this is where the real damage comes from. Stay close and let them stack.
